Our Story


Dan and Ashley met during their first semester at SUNY Oneonta in the fall of 2008. They were both enrolled in Sociology 101. They sat across from each other in class and Dan remembers admiring Ashley from a far but not having the courage to move his seat closer. It wasn’t until they saw each other at a classy Oneonta house party that they became friends. Although the attraction between them was undeniable, they were both having so much fun during their first year of college they weren’t ready to get into a serious relationship.

It wasn’t until Dan’s senior year and Ashley’s junior year at SUNY Oneonta, that the two made it official. Dan called Ashley over the summer and told her that he wanted to make her his girlfriend and couldn’t wait any longer for them to be together. Ashley of course said yes—but had to play hard to get—so the two became a couple on October 4th, 2010.

Dan and Ashley enjoyed spending time together as a couple during Dan’s last year at SUNY Oneonta. When Dan graduated he was offered an amazing opportunity to work at Rosetta Digital Marketing in Cleveland, Ohio and their relationship was put to the test. Dan and Ashley dated long distance during Ashley’s last year of college. Even though they were miles apart they stayed in love and committed to each other, making sure to visit see each other—using planes, trains, and automobiles—at least once a month.

Upon graduation, Ashley was inducted into Teach For America as a corps member and placed in Cleveland, Ohio! The happy couple was reunited in Cleveland and enjoyed being together in the same city. In July of 2013, Ashley and Dan moved into their first apartment together in Lakewood, Ohio where they lived for one year.

Once Ashley completed her corps member experience, she accepted a position at KIPP Academy Elementary school in the South Bronx. Dan was able to transfer to Rosetta’s New York office and the couple relocated to Manhattan in July of 2014. Dan and Ashley enjoyed exploring all that the Big Apple has to offer from Central Park to Times Square, museums to restaurants, Broadway to subways!

On July 3rd, 2015 Dan asked Ashley to accompany him on a trip to their favorite place in the city—Central Park. He arranged a gourmet picnic lunch and found a secluded spot in the park, by the lake, for the two to eat. After lunch, Dan asked Ashley to take a picture facing the lake. When she turned around, Dan was on one knee asking for her hand in marriage. Ashley of course said yes—but at first said “WHAT?!” because she was completely surprised and extremely happy!

Dan and Ashley are so excited to see where their journey and marriage will lead them, what new cities and opportunities they will explore, and most importantly how much their love will grow in the years to come! They are thrilled to be getting married, on their engagement anniversary, July 3rd, 2016!
